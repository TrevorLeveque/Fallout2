How to prepare for the inevitable!

Communism is taking over America! So you can't be too careful with your families lives when Nuclear Winter inevitably comes.

Getting a fallout shelter is a must when the nukes come raining down. It must be a very accessible fallout shelter than you can get too quickly when you hear those air raid sirens.

Start picking through your gold old American coins to be looking for copper, silver and anything else that will be useful to you once the dollar bill becomes worthless.

Get some type of armory so you can defend yourself. Those friendly neighbors won't be so friendly when they hardly have a brain!

  • Lots and lots of water
